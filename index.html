The Heart of Himmafushi Island, Maldives.

We are located at the center of Himmafushi, North Male' Atoll in Maldives. The Island is approximately 9.6Km away from Airport, only 20 minutes of boat ride away. 
The Island features a beautiful beach and surrounded by beautiful Lagoon, and also the friendly Locals who are always known for their hospitality cannot be forgotten. The Island hosts the famous surf break "Jailbreaks" which can be reached by walk.
